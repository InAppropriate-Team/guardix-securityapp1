Privacy Policy – Guardix Security

At Guardix Security, safeguarding your privacy is a top priority. This document explains what information we collect, how it is used, and the measures we apply to protect your data when you use our mobile app and related services. By accessing Guardix Security, you consent to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

a. Technical and Device Data

When using the app, we may automatically gather non-personally identifiable technical details, such as:

  • - your device type and hardware specifications;
  • - operating system version;
  • - unique device identifiers (e.g., IMEI);
  • - network details, IP and MAC addresses;
  • - the version of the app installed;;
  • - crash diagnostics and performance logs.

This data is essential to ensure stable operation across devices and to troubleshoot technical issues.


b. Information You Provide

When interacting with the app (e.g., sending feedback or using features), we may receive:

  • - images or files processed within the app;
  • - support requests, messages, or comments;
  • - any content you voluntarily upload.

These inputs help us improve services, assist users, and reduce misuse.


c. Access to Media and Camera

Guardix Security may request permission to use your camera or photo library for specific features, such as:

  • - image-cleaning tools;
  • - submitting visual feedback or diagnostics.

Granting this access is optional and only occurs with your explicit approval. We neither store nor transmit personal media outside your device.

2. How We Use the Data

Collected information may be applied to:

  • - maintain proper app functionality and compatibility;
  • - deliver updates or important notices;
  • - respond to customer inquiries;
  • - personalize the user experience;
  • - investigate crashes or malfunctions;
  • - detect potential fraud or misuse;
  • - assess the performance of promotions or ads.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not trade or sell personal details. In limited, secure cases, data may be shared, for example:

  • -with trusted partners for payment or refund troubleshooting;
  • - in anonymized form to analyze performance and improve stability.

Any data shared is handled securely and strictly for operational needs.

4. Changes to the Policy

From time to time, this Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect new features, compliance obligations, or user suggestions. Major revisions will be communicated through the app or other suitable channels. Please review this policy periodically to remain informed.

5. Data Protection

We apply appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to secure your information, including SSL/TLS encryption for all communications. Access to Google user data is restricted to authorized staff who require it for service operation. We never sell or disclose Google user data to third parties. All records are stored on secure servers with industry-standard protections.

6. Retention and Deletion

Google user data is retained only as long as necessary for service delivery or as required by law. Once no longer needed, information is permanently deleted or anonymized. Users may request deletion of their Google data at any time by contacting us at info@guardix-security.com . Such requests will be fulfilled within a reasonable timeframe unless legal retention is required.

7. Access to Google Data

The app integrates with Google services to provide essential functionality. In particular, we request access to:

  • -Gmail (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.modify ) — enabling the app to read and manage Gmail messages, including listing emails, updating labels, and removing unwanted messages.

How this data is used:

  • - to let users view and manage their Gmail inbox within the app;
  • - to perform cleaning functions such as moving or deleting messages for better organization.

What we do not do:

  • - we do not store Gmail message content on our servers;
  • - we do not share Gmail data with outside parties;
  • - we do not use Gmail information for marketing, ads, or any purpose beyond the stated functions.

Users may revoke access at any time in their Google Account settings.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding your privacy or how your data is managed, please contact us:

Email: info@guardix-security.com
